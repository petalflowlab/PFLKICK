The Contact Staff

The Petal Staff. V2.

Built From Scratch. Ready to Be Built Right.

Every part of the Petal Staff was designed from the ground up — the petal heads, the electronics, the assembly. It has been reborn and torn apart and is ready for a more stable life in an LED space with you.

🌸 Silicone Molded Petals

The 3D printed petals have held up beautifully — smashes, drops, contact staff abuse, poi life — they've taken it all but larger dragon petals are a different story. For some spinners they've met their breaking point, which was always the risk with that size.

The good news? They're easily swapped out. But for V2 I'm looking to survive it all. Silicone molded petals will be necessary. More robust, more reliable, and built to keep up with the hard knock life of a flow prop.

📡 Motion-Sensitive LEDs

A custom PCB with a 3-axis accelerometer and gyroscope tracks your exact speed, tilt, and rotation in real time. The LEDs respond to what you're actually doing — not a preset loop. Fast spin triggers a bloom. A slow roll closes it back down. It knows the difference.

Testing has revealed only minor issues but great durability on impacts. Layout needs a slight revision and with a complete rework of LED connection point was needed to keep the LEDS secure and wiggle free.

📏 3-Part Collapsible Design

A 3 piece design was needed. This helped with the motion tracking with a sensor on both ends, portability, and battery life/size.

The Latest Evolution

Enter the Flow Sword.

1 Capsule. More potential.

I've been looking to get more flow out of my contact sword. The capsule can power both the staff and sword variants. The best part is that the sword construction is so so simple and resilient. The petals help with balance for easy fishtails without weighing the base too much.

⚒️ Sturdy & Reliable Build

Designed for resilience. The blade is a high-grade polycarbonate tube housing a double-sided LED strip for maximum visibility. The motion-tracking capsule sits safely within the polycarbonate hilt, making the entire prop stiff, sturdy, and extremely reliable.

It's a simple, elegant assembly that's built to take the abuse of contact sword play and high-speed strikes.

  • ✔️ Blade: 80cm polycarbonate
  • ✔️ Hilt: 50cm — capsule housing
  • ✔️ Head: Mechanical Petal Bloom

Petal Balance

The sword is balanced as a hybrid with minimal extra weight ends. Fish tails are possible but not super slow as with a full contact sword. A weight attachment can quickly move it's balance to behave liek a tradtional contact sword.

The flowers help with balance and makes the end weight feel less neeeded while giving a much swifter moveement thatn full contact. the petals offer even more stability when spun liek a mini dragon. LEDS are high quality strips rather than onboard so they can resist impact form drops.
